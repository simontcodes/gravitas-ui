# Foundations

Foundations are the core design decisions behind Gravitas UI.

They define the visual language that keeps components consistent across every product.

---

## What lives in Foundations?

### Colors

Your semantic and brand color tokens.

### Typography

Font sizes, weights, line heights, and text hierarchy.

### Spacing

The spacing scale used for padding, gaps, and layout rhythm.

### Radius

Border radius values for inputs, cards, modals, and buttons.

### Shadows

Elevation styles for floating and layered UI.

### Motion

Animation timing and interaction behavior.

### Themes

Global appearance modes such as light and dark.

### Density

Global spacing density such as compact, comfortable, and spacious.

---

## Why Foundations matter

Without foundations, components become inconsistent very quickly.

Foundations make sure that:

- buttons and inputs feel related
- spacing looks intentional
- typography stays readable
- themes remain predictable
- new components are easier to build
